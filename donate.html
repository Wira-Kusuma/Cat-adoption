Donation

You can help street cats by donating to our program. We encourage street feeders who feed at the colonies that we monitor to contribute. Because feeding without getting them fixed will cause them to breed faster.

Your donations will be allocated to help sick cats, deworming and flea medicines, sterilization which includes hospitalization and transport. We will do our best to ensure all cats are in good health before they are spayed or neutered and released.

We also prioritize female cats if we see female cats that are ready to be spayed.
