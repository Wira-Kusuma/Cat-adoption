Program

Donation

You can donate to help street cats through TNR (Trap Neuter/Spay Return). Donations will be allocated for sterilization including hospitalization and transportation, fleas and worms treatment, and medication for sick cats.

Adoption

You can help by adopting or fostering at least one street cat. We need a safe home and a family with commitment who are trusted and responsible to care for the cats, especially, those with special needs, kittens, sick cats, and senior cats.

Education

You can share useful information so that a lot of people can help street cats and become responsible carers. We invite you to share your experiences and stories, as well as useful information/posters on our website and social media.
