About

Catrescue.id is an initiative that aims to help street cats through a sterilization program called Trap Neuter Return (TNR), especially for cats that live in a cat colony in a public space that we monitor.

Due to limited resources, we conduct TNR activities in only 3 cat colonies that are each in the Depok, Menteng and Tanjung Barat.

We also look for adopters for cats from the colony with special needs such as kittens, pregnant cats, sick cats or senior cats, or cats that live in a dangerous location.

We tried to gather people who care to help our TNR program, coordinate in feeding and monitoring the health of the cats that live in the colonies.

Catrescue.id also seeks to disseminate information about sterilization and how to care for cats properly, through websites and social media.
